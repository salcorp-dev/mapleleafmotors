# v10.9 Final Biweekly Display

Update:
- The edit modal now always shows the final biweekly based on the current calculator inputs.
- Clicking Save Vehicle automatically recalculates before saving, so the saved biweekly matches the selected APR, term, tax, price, and down payment.
- Public inventory cards are patched to show the final saved biweekly payment when `biweeklyPayment` exists.

Calculator:
- 12% Manitoba tax
- Standard amortization
- APR options from v10.8
- Term options from v10.8
- Biweekly = monthly × 12 ÷ 26
- Rounded to nearest dollar
